Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-11

Summary

No account required

You can use the app without signing up.

Local-first

Your orders are stored on your device.

No selling of data

The app does not sell personal data.

No ads / tracking

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs or tracking.

Data the app processes

Creator’s Orderbook helps you track orders and may store information you enter, such as:

  • Order title and notes
  • Client name (optional)
  • Deadlines and order status
  • Payment amounts (total, advance paid) and currency

This data is stored locally on your device to provide the core functionality of the app.

Data collection

Creator’s Orderbook does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated.

Permissions

The app does not require special permissions to function. If a future feature requires a permission (for example, photo access for attachments), the app will request it explicitly and only when needed.

Data retention & deletion

Because data is stored on your device:

  • You can delete an order inside the app.
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app from your device.

Children’s privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If the app’s privacy practices change, this Privacy Policy may be updated. The “Effective date” at the top reflects the latest version.
